Quest Guide: X Marks the Spot

Zone: Lionhold NPC: Zorrin of Fenn Requirement: 1 Silver

Speak with Zorrin of Fenn in Lionhold to begin X Marks the Spot. Have at least 1 Silver to receive the quest.

Steps

  1. Meet Zorrin of Fenn in Lionhold to start the quest.
  2. Travel to the Goblin Ruins (Ruins of Aela).
  3. Interact with the shovel at the marked spot to complete the objective.
  4. Return to Zorrin to turn in the quest for rewards.

Visual Guide

Rewards

2 Silver
